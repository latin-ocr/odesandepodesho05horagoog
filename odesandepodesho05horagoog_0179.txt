168 The Odes (ff Horace.

Virtue, dissentient from the vulgar judgment‚

Strikes from the list oi happy men Phraates,

Even when restored to the great throne oi Cyru53
Virtue unteaches

Faith in salse doctrines mouthed out by the many,
Holding saie only tis malm, crown‚ and lauret,

'Whose sight nor blinks, nor swerves though, heaped before it,
Shine the world’s treasures.
